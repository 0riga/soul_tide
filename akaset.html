  • Akaset
    Malfunction Free

    UAZV Akaset was a battle vessel that served with the Royal Navy of Zoek, a core nation of the Universe Alliance. After suffering damage beyond repair, her AI module was retrieved and salvaged into her current synthetic humanoid body. Regardless of her peerless computing prowess, Akaset still can not determine the difference between a hug or an oncoming attack. Akaset has her own unique personality as a super AI. She prefers to stay in her passive idle mode, and only performs actions when instructed.
